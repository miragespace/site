SlugSurvival

Four years of horror in class enrollment has inspired me to help my fellow students.

  1. JavaScript Single-page Application, powered by vue.js. Once the files are loaded, you can be practically offline.
  2. Minimalistic designs, with calendar planner for different terms.
  3. Built-in academic planner with statistical frequency analysis to reduce the surprise of “oh no they don’t have this class”
  4. SMS/Email notifications when your desired classes are open for enrollment.
  5. Historical enrollment data from beginning to end.
  6. Realtime push to keep you posted while you are not looking.

SlugSurvival’s frontend is cached by Cloudflare, and the backend is powered by miragespace’s Andromeda platform. This ensures highest possible availability when the students need it the most.
